The Additional Member Form should collect the general information of additional household members.

Any personal data collected will be added to individual user profile in Information field.

## Required Information 

* First Name

* Last Name

* Date of Birth 

* Gender 

* Demographic (Senior, Signal Parent or Indigenous)

## Optional Information

* Identity (MCP)

## Submit Form

After filled all the required information, data can be saved to the information field by clicking the `Save` button.
